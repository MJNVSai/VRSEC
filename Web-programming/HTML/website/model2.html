MODEL:   Mercedes-Benz S-Class



Mercedes-Benz has launched the new-gen S-Class in the country. The model, which arrives in the country through the CBU route. And it is available in two variants that include the S450 and S400d. Exterior highlights of the 2021 Mercedes-Benz S-Class include sweptback LED headlamps. A new grille with three horizontal slats, new 20-inch alloy wheels, and wedge-shaped LED tail lights. The model is available in five colours including Designo Diamond White,Onyx Black, Anthracite Blue, Rubellite Red, and Emrald Green.
